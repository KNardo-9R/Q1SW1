Violet Evergarden

"It is a pleasure to meet you. If it is your wish, I will travel anywhere to meet your request. Auto Memory Doll, Violet Evergarden, at your service."

A masterpiece by Kana Akatsuki originally published under Kyoto Animation on 2015, Violet Evergarden is a delight of a light novel series which was adapted as an anime in 2018 that tells the story of a young girl, ex-military, trying to find her way in life as a ghostwriter after the war. Despite nearly a decade since its original release, the story stands as a testament to time as one that solemnly touches encumbered and healthy hearts alike up until today.


A Series Part of Me

Why Do I Love It?

  • It's a beautiful series with sentimental and meaningful moments wonderfully packed into the episodes---it's far from a waste of time.
  • Attachment to the characters in the series is inevitable: They're all incredibly well-written.
  • The visuals of the series shine unbridled; every frame emanates the care of the artists, and as a result, each scene pulls you in.
